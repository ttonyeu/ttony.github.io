Thomas Tony

I am a first-year PhD student in pure mathematics at the Mathematical Institute of the University of Münster. I am a member of the Custer of Excellence "Mathematics Münster: Dynamics – Geometry – Structure" and the Mathematics Münster Graduate School. My supervisor is Rudolf Zeidler.

Research

My main research interests lies in the study of positive scalar curvature metrics, spin geometry, Dirac operators, index theory and K-theory for C*-algebras. In my PhD project I am studying Riemannian spin manifolds with lower scalar curvature bounds and certain rigidity results for non-vanishing Rosenberg index. Therefore, I am combining spinorial techniques, where the considered Dirac operator acts on Hilbert modules over suitable C*-algebras, with functional analytic methods.
