I am a first-year PhD student in pure mathematics at the Mathematical Institute of the University of Münster. I am a member of the Cluster of Excellence "Mathematics Münster: Dynamics – Geometry – Structure" and the Mathematics Münster Graduate School. My supervisor is Rudolf Zeidler.

Research

My main research interests lie in the study of positive scalar curvature metrics, spin geometry, Dirac operators, index theory and K-theory for C*-algebras. In my PhD project I am studying Riemannian spin manifolds with lower scalar curvature bounds and certain rigidity results for non-vanishing Rosenberg index. Thereby, I am combining analytic methods with spinor techniques with Dirac operators acting on Hilbert modules over C*-algebras.
