About me

I am a PhD student in pure mathematics at the Mathematical Institute of the University of Münster. I am a member of the Cluster of Excellence "Mathematics Münster: Dynamics – Geometry – Structure" and the CRC 1442 "Geometry: Deformations and Rigidity". My supervisor is Rudolf Zeidler.

Research

My main research interests lie in the study of positive scalar curvature metrics, spin geometry, Dirac operators, index theory and K-theory for C*-algebras. In my PhD project I am studying Riemannian spin manifolds with lower scalar curvature bounds and certain rigidity results under non-vanishing Rosenberg index. Thereby, I am combining analytical methods with spinorial techniques with Dirac operators acting on Hilbert C*-modules.
