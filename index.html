Thomas Tony


Welcome! My name is Thomas Tony. I am a first-year PhD student in pure mathematics at the University Münster supervised by Rudolf Zeidler. My position is funded by Germany’s Excellence Strategy EXC 2044–390685587, Mathematics Münster: Dynamics–Geometry–Structure.

Research

My main research interests lies in the study of positive scalar curvature metrics, spin geometry, Dirac operators, index theory and K-theory for C*-algebras. In my PhD project I am studying Riemannian spin manifolds with lower scalar curvature bounds and certain rigidity results assuming non-triviality of the Rosenberg index. In the course of this I am using spinorial techniques, where the considered Dirac operator acts on Hilbert modules over C*-algebras.
